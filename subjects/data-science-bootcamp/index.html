About This Bootcamp

This bootcamp is designed to prepare you for Data Scientist roles in the Finnish job market. It covers six comprehensive study plans based on analysis of real job postings from major Finnish companies including Nokia, Wolt, Reaktor, Supercell, and others.

Unlike the structured 44-week learning journey, this bootcamp is self-paced and flexible. Complete each study plan at your own speed, marking them as complete when you're ready. Each plan includes detailed week-by-week guidance, practical exercises, and assessment checklists.
